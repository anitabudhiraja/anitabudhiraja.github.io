About Me

Bridging Academia
& Industry

I am Anita Budhiraja — a dedicated educator, researcher, and technology leader with over 30 years of transformative experience in Computer Science and Emerging Technologies, currently serving as Scientist-E, Associate Dean – Academics, and Head of Department (Training) at NIELIT Chandigarh/Ropar.

My work spans Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Blockchain, IoT Security, Data Science, and AR/VR — areas where I have led curriculum design, expert lectures, research publications, and Government of India flagship projects.

As Chief Investigator for FutureSkills PRIME (MeitY, Govt. of India), I lead national upskilling initiatives in Big Data & Data Science and AR/VR for IT professionals across India.
